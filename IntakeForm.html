Denial
Payer received and processed the claim but refused payment. Includes CO, PR, and OA denial codes. Appeal deadlines apply.
❌
Rejection
Claim was never processed — returned due to a technical or formatting error. Must be corrected and resubmitted.
💰
Payment Issue
Claim was paid but the amount is incorrect. Use for underpayments, overpayments, or contractual disputes.
⚙️
Internal Issue
Internal workflow or system problem — batch errors, duplicate policies, wrong clearinghouse, missing NPI in batch.
